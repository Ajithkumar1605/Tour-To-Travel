Popular Destinations

KERALA

Kerala, located on the southwestern coast of India, is known for its stunning landscapes, lush greenery, and rich cultural heritage. Often referred to as "God's Own Country", it features serene backwaters, beautiful beaches, and the Western Ghats mountain range. Kerala is famous for its Ayurvedic treatments, vibrant festivals, and traditional art forms like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam. The cuisine is diverse and flavorful, with dishes like appam and fish curry being popular. With its unique blend of nature and culture, Kerala attracts tourists seeking relaxation and adventure alike.

From $ 400

LONDON

London, the capital of the United Kingdom, is a vibrant city known for its rich history, diverse culture, and iconic landmarks. Key attractions include the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, the British Museum, and the London Eye. The city is a major financial center and offers a wide range of arts, theater, and culinary experiences. With its extensive public transport system and multicultural atmosphere, London is a dynamic hub for both residents and visitors.

From $ 800

THAILAND

Thailand, located in Southeast Asia, is known for its stunning beaches, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant cities. Popular destinations include Bangkok, with its bustling markets and temples, and islands like Phuket and Koh Samui, famous for their crystal-clear waters. Thai cuisine is celebrated globally, featuring flavors like spicy, sour, and sweet. The country is also known for its warm hospitality and festivals, such as Songkran (Thai New Year) and Loy Krathong, making it a favorite destination for travelers seeking adventure and relaxation.

From $ 2000

MANALI

Manali is a picturesque hill station in Himachal Pradesh, India, nestled in the Himalayas. Known for its stunning landscapes, lush valleys, and snow-capped peaks, it’s a popular destination for adventure sports like trekking, skiing, and paragliding. Key attractions include the Solang Valley, Rohtang Pass, and Hadimba Temple. Manali's vibrant culture, pleasant climate, and charming markets make it a favorite getaway for both nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

From $ 1900

AMERICA

The United States, often referred to as America, is a diverse country known for its vast landscapes, cultural variety, and economic influence. It features iconic cities like New York, Los Angeles, and Chicago, each with unique attractions. The U.S. is famous for its national parks, such as Yellowstone and the Grand Canyon, showcasing its natural beauty. Its rich history, democratic values, and cultural contributions in music, film, and technology make it a significant player on the global stage.

From $ 650

MUMBAI

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is the financial capital of India and one of its largest cities. Located on the west coast, it’s known for its vibrant culture, bustling markets, and iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India and Marine Drive. Mumbai is a melting pot of diverse communities and cuisines, famous for its street food and Bollywood film industry. The city's blend of modern skyscrapers and historic architecture, along with its lively nightlife, makes it a dynamic and exciting destination.

From $ 2300
